Things to try
  • All three to 255 → white (all light!)
  • All three to 0 → black (no light)
  • Max red + max green, no blue → yellow
  • Max red + max blue, no green → magenta
  • Max green + max blue, no red → cyan
  • Equal amounts of all three → gray
